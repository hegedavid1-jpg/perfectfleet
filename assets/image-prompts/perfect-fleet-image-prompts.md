# Perfect Fleet - képgenerálási promptok

Ezek a promptok a főoldal képhelyeihez készültek. Minden képnél fontos: nincs látható autómárka logó, nincs rendszám, nincs szöveg, nincs watermark.

## 1. Hero kép

Fájlnév: `hero-modern-fleet.jpg`

Prompt:

```text
Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: website homepage hero image for a business car leasing and fleet management company
Primary request: Create a premium photorealistic hero image of a modern silver company car, close-up front three-quarter angle, in a bright minimalist fleet delivery space.
Scene/backdrop: clean architectural showroom or corporate vehicle handover area, light gray and white surfaces, subtle depth, professional B2B atmosphere.
Subject: modern unbranded sedan or SUV, no visible manufacturer logos, no license plate text.
Style/medium: photorealistic commercial automotive photography.
Composition/framing: wide landscape image, car dominates the left side, enough clean negative space on the right for website headline text.
Lighting/mood: soft daylight, crisp reflections, polished but not flashy.
Color palette: white, silver, graphite, small warm orange reflection accents matching #ff8914.
Constraints: no text, no watermark, no people, no readable logos, no license plate details.
Avoid: dramatic dark studio, racing mood, luxury excess, old vehicle, busy background.
```
